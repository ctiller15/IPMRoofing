Who are we?

IPM Roofing provides roofing services to Valrico, FL and surrounding areas. IPM Roofing, is a privately owned and operated company. Mr. Aftene Iacob, started in the metal roofing trade (at the age of 18). Now, at the age of 50, he is one of the most skilled metal roofing craftsmen in the United States! Mr. Iacob is very proud to be present on EVERY job that IPM Roofing is contracted to install.
